* **Choose the Crossbody Padded Bag** if you want to commute completely unencumbered by extra packs while carrying your console, wallet, phone, and standard accessories safely.
* **Choose the Deluxe Suitcase** if you frequently travel between hotel rooms, tournament venues, or friend's houses and need to bring the *entire* dock setup securely.
